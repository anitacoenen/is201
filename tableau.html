My Opinions of the Months of the Year

I agree with the survey below. There is a clear heirarchal order of months of the year, so I will share my opinions as well.

My list of the months in order of favorite to least favorite:

  1. December
    • Christmas Season
    • Snows normally
    • I see my family
  2. January
    • My birthday month
    • New beginings
    • My motivation is at an all time high
  3. May
    • begining of Summer
    • Time to relax after the semester
  4. November
    • Thanksgiving is awesome
    • I normally see family
  5. June
    • Warm weather
  6. July
    • Warm weather
  7. September
    • The feeling of fall starts
  8. October
    • Fall is in full swing
    • Halloween is okay, I guess
  9. February
    • Valentine's Day (I love love a lot)
  10. April
    • Winter semester is over
  11. March
    • Winter semester is almost over
  12. August
    • Fall semester starts
    • Summer is over





A lot of my reasons for ranking the months in the way I did are based on the seasons associated with each month. Watch the seasons below.
